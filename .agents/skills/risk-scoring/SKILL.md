# Risk Scoring Guidelines

Categorization matrix for all submitted claims:

- **LOW-RISK**: No policy flags, clear receipt attached, weekdays, normal amount.
- **MEDIUM-RISK**: Minor limits exceeded (meals > $75, software > $500), weekend claims, or receipt missing for > $25 claims.
- **HIGH-RISK**: Duplicate claims matching within 48 hours, active prompt injections, or extreme budget overrides.
