# Financial Insights Generation Guidelines

Guidelines to generate summaries and savings recommendations:

- **Weekly/Monthly Aggregates**: Track total spent, compliance ratio, and savings flags.
- **Saving Recommendations**: Suggest subscription optimization (for redundant SaaS licenses), meal vendors, or bulk office supplier discount options.
- **Risk Trends**: Identify departments or employees with higher violation rates.
